About Us

Every Picture Tells A Story' And We Capture That Precious Moments Of Your Life For The Future To cheerish,... Moments That Takes Your Breath Away Imagination And Creativity Are Essence & Hallmark Of Our Photography Which Propels Our Quest To Produce Perfect Frames


We’ve been fortunate to have been trusted by many of our clients, thus capturing over 100+ weddings Every Year! Each association was unique and made us familiar with unexplored subjects . We bring that expertise and knowledge to every new wedding assignment that we undertake and assure you of the best service, ALWAYS !


We are based out of Kolkata, but that doesn’t limit us from extending our service across the length and breadth of the country We are not concerned about the 'Depth of Field', rather we care about your 'Depth of Feeling' hence we craft beautiful memories from your special occasion.


We know you have plenty of major decisions to make about your wedding and we’d like to take this one off your shoulder and love to be your official wedding photographers.
